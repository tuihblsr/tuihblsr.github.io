Main Chronological Order Rando

It's the year 2110. Humanity discovered nanotechnology, which led to an unprecedented golden age. For a while at least. Turns out nanotech was pretty power intensive, and and our current infastructure couldn't keep up. As the years passed, power shortages became a regular issue. Countries waged war on each other, fighting over our dwindling power supplies. Eventually, they fell apart and reformed into the factions that we know of today. Space travel is possible, but it's confined to our own solar system and is tightly controlled.

Name: The Silver Claws
Main Territories: Sector 21, 22, 23
The most technologically advanced faction in the world. They are responsible for many of the world's inventions, including nanotech. They consider traditional ballistic weaponry, like firing shells and projectiles, an uncivilised, crude method of warfare. They instead rely on energy based weaponry and nanotech. If a design can be improved in any way, they are quick to do it.
