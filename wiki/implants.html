Below are the implants that Silver Claw members usually get, and what they do.



Neural Interface Implant

A decentralized cranial implant that allows the user to remotely connect to the internet with a single thought. It is required for other implants and cybernetic limbs to function properly. The implant is installed by releasing millions of nanobots into the human brain, each of which attaches to a different neural pathway.

Sight

A bionic eye implant that grants sight capabilities far beyond those of a natural human. Embedded circuitry within the implant provides the ability to detect infrared light.
