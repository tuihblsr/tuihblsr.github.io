The main headquarters of the Silver Claws was a giant space station rotating around mars. All of their main descisions are made HERE. It is defended by a dome-like shield called "The Wall".

The Wall

The wall is effecitvely a massive bubble that surrounds the station. It is made up of millions of teardrop-shaped drones, each the size of a fingernail. When a spaceship flies close, the drones get slightly displaced but quickly return to their positions, using tiny engines. When individual drones begin to run out of energy, they seek out their neighbors and draw power from them. Larger "nurse" drones cruise along, providing additional power to random drones, which then distrubute the power along.
