The main headquarters of the Silver Claws was a giant space station rotating around mars. All of their main descisions are made HERE. It is defended by a dome-like shield called "The Wall".

The Wall

The wall is effecitvely a massive bubble that surrounds the station. It's actually made up of millions of tiny, fingernail-sized drones. They're shaped like teardrops and have tiny engines in them that allow to stay in place. When a spaceship flies near them, they get buffeted back and the bubble deforms a bit, but eventually they'll all work their way back into place. Enough maneuvering, and the drones battery would run down. When this happened, it fly over and touch it's neighbor, and the weaker would draw power from the stronger. There were also larger drones called nurses that would cruise along randomly dumping large amounts of power into a drone, which would then distribute it to it's neighbors.
