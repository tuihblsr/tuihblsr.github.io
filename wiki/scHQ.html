The Wall

The wall is effectively a massive bubble that surrounds the station. It is made up of millions of teardrop-shaped drones (called clawstats), each the size of a fingernail. When a spaceship flies close, the drones can sometimes can buffeted around but will quickly return to their positions via tiny engines. When individual drones begin to run out of energy, they seek out their neighbors and draw power from them. Larger "nurse" drones cruise along, providing additional power to random drones, which then distrubute the power along. If a drone is damaged or experiencing problems, it will send out a distress signal, prompting a replacement drone to take its place.

Whenever an unauthorized object can comes near the bubble, the drones will fire high-velocity photons, vaporising the target on contact.
