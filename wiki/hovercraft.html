Hovercraft are essentially flying cars designed to glide silently through the air. Unlike traditional vehicles, they have the unique ability to hover or fly-similar to helicopters or drones-but without the need for large rotating blades or jets.

They do this by manipulating gravity in a way that pushes/pulls the craft in various directions. This, however, is incredibly energy intensive and so as a result most hovercraft have a very limited flight time.
