Main Chronological Order Rando

1. Jules joins The Silver Claws

• 1050 words

Nell had outgrown her old clothes, and Jules promised to help her get new ones. She trailed behind her into the kitchen, her eyes wide as they landed on a massive contraption sitting in the corner. It looked like a microwave, but bigger. “What's that?” Nell asked, curious.

Jules grinned, "It's called a matter assembler. It's how we get anything we need, from clothes to tools."

"If it can make anything, then why doesn't everyone use it?"

"Because it uses a lot of energy," Jules replied, "And not everyone has access to enough power for it. It's a bit of a luxury, really." She gives the matter assembler a dramatic poke, and a loading screen pops up.
