1. Jules joins The Silver Claws

• 1050 words

Nell had outgrown her old clothes, and Jules promised to help her get new ones. She trailed behind her into the kitchen, her eyes wide as they landed on a massive contraption sitting in the corner. It looked like a microwave, but bigger. “What's that?” Nell asked, curious.

Jules grinned, "It's called a matter assembler. It's how we get anything we need, from clothes to tools."

"If it can make anything, then why doesn't everyone use it?"

"Well," Jules said, tapping the side of the machine, "to make things, it needs basic molecules-the building blocks of everything. For example, to make water, it needs hydrogen and oxygen atoms. To make a shirt, it needs carbon, hydrogen, and a few other elements. The problem is, you need the right atoms, and those take a lot of energy to gather and put together. Not everyone has enough energy or access to them. We're lucky that we have access to the Feed Network." She gave the matter assembler a dramatic poke, and a loading screen popped up, along with the picture of a shirt.
